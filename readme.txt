Show your beautiful AdPage campaigns on your WordPress website without the need of another domain name.

== Description ==

Show your beautiful AdPage campaigns on your WordPress website without the need of another domain name.

All you need to do is sign-up ad AdPage.io and create a new campaign.

With our simple cloud-based editor, you're able to create perfect campaigns within just a few minutes. Increase potential customers with landingpages.

Drag and drop blocks and modify them to your needs.

AdPage offers multiple integrations to services such as MailChimp and ActiveCampaign. 

<b>Notice!</b>
This plugin connects to our servers to download the campaigns you made to your own website. We don't send anything else to us.

== Installation ==

Download the plugin from the WordPress plugin directory or search for beta releases on GitHub.

After installation, enter your AdPage API-key which can be created on your dashboard.
(https://app.adpage.io/dashboard/settings).

To connect campaigns, you need to publish them first. We'll help you with that in the AdPage app itself.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I create the API-key? =

Login on your dashboard on https://app.adpage.io/ and click on "settings", here you're able to generate the key. Simply paste it and you're good to go!
